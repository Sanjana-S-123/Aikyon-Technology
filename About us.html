About Us

Our Expertise:.

We are a team of passionate Engineers with over a Decade of Experience & recognized presence in VLSI design and services. Our Engineers have led and delivered multiple highly complex Projects across different nodes from 180nm to cutting Edge 3nm Technologies & multi GHz designs. We have worked with Companies like AMD, Broadcom, MediaTek, Intel, Innovium Technologies, Marvell technologies, Microsoft, ST Micro, SanDisk, Qualcomm, Samsung & ST Micro to name few. Our Area of expertise lies in but not limited to SOC Physical Design Architecture, Synthesis, Place and Route, Static Timing Analysis (STA) & Various Other signoff flows.
